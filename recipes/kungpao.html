Explosively Fragrant: Kung Pao Chicken

The explosively delicious Kung Pao chicken!

Fancy something fast? Even faster using a Just Eat delivery bike? Kung Pao chicken (or UberEat) is what you are thinking about.

List of ingredients:

  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Chilli
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Carrots
  • Onion
  • Chicken breast

Steps:

  1. Marinate the chicken breast
  2. Make your own Kung Pao sauce by mixing appropriate amount of soy sauce, vinegar and sugar
  3. Stir fry the vegetables followed by chicken breast
  4. Add the Kung Pao sauce
  5. Allow simmering for 15 mins
  6. Enjoy your explosively good chicken!
