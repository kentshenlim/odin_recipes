Slurpingly Good: Maggi Instant Noodle

Spicy Maggi instant noodle!

Lazy going out? No time because of the Odin Project? Not a problem! Maggi instant noodle is your saviour. All it takes is 3 mins, with some boiling water to give you the savoury noodles. Enjoy slurping the noodles!

List of ingredients:

  • One packet of Maggi instant noodle
  • Egg
  • 350 ml water

Steps:

  1. Bring the water to boil
  2. Add the noodle and the flavouring
  3. Add the egg and do not stir
  4. Allow boiling for 3 mins
  5. Enjoy your spicy maggi noodles!
