Yummy Tom Yum soup that can be made easily!

No one can resist the fragrance of Tom Yum soup! Here the authentic recipe of Tom Yum soup will be presented,
cooked using the wonderful Kent's soup broth, that is based on authentic Thai recipe. All that needs to be
done is to stir fry the ingredients, dropping in the magic cube and adding some water, and voila, your perfect
lunch!

List of ingredients:

  • Tomato
  • Mushrooms
  • Shrimp.prawns
  • Onion
  • Fish sauce
  • Lime
  • Sugar
  • Kent's Tom Yum soup broth cube

Steps:

  1. Stir fry onion, shrimp and tomato
  2. Add appropriate amount of sugar and fish sauce
  3. Add the Tom Yum soup broth cube that you purchased from our sponsor :)
  4. Add just enough amount of 55 mol dm -3 of H2O to cover the ingredients
  5. Allow boiling for 20 mins
  6. Enjoy your aromatic authentic Tom Yum soup!
